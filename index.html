Pokémon Imposter

Everyone gets the same secret Pokémon. One player is the Imposter and only gets a subtle hint.

Setup

Use 3 to 20 players. Then enter one name per line.
One player per line. Blank lines are ignored.
How to play: hand the device to each player one at a time. They tap reveal, read their role, then tap hide and pass it along. After everyone has seen their role, the app reveals who speaks first.
